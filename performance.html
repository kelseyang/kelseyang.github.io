Dance and Performance

Kelsey has had the pleasure of performing Bill T. Jones, Sidra Bell, and BODYTRAFFIC repertoire, and working with top LA choreographers/ companies - CARLON Company (Jay Carlon), Toogie Barcelo and Invertigo Dance Theatre. On screen, she is in a music video for Lee Grove Music (Kristen Gutoskie) and a Blenders Eyewear campaign.
