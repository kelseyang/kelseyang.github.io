Dancing Through Parkinson's Teaching Artist

As a Teaching Artist for Invertigo Dance Theatre’s Dancing Through Parkinson’s (DTP) program, Kelsey leads dance classes for people with Parkinson’s Disease and other neurological challenges. These classes encourage joy, community, connection and physical movement.

In addition to live classes, Kelsey has taught for Invertigo’s full-length, full-of-heart online DTP classes. These classes have been featured in press, such as LA Times, LA Dance Chronicle and Broadway World, and enjoyed by many from their homes.
